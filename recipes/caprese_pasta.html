Caprese pasta

Description:

I love making this pasta recipe all year round, but I especially love it when I have fresh basil and cherry tomatoes in the garden. Thereâ€™s no better way to put it to good use than with this amazing recipe.

The tomato cream sauce is so flavorful, and has a combination of Parmesan and melty mozzarella, which pairs perfectly with fresh tomatoes and basil. Juicy seared chicken makes it a complete meal, which you can even drizzle with a little balsamic glaze for the ultimate Caprese flair!

Ingredients:

  • 1 large boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • Salt/Pepper
  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps:

  1. Prep Work: Combine the half and half, chicken broth, and seasonings
  2. Cook the Chicken: Cut the chicken in half lengthwise to create 2-3 thinner slices.
  3. Begin to heat a pot of salted pasta water while you begin to prepare the sauce as outlined below.
  4. Add the wine to the same skillet that you used to cook the chicken and set the heat to medium.
  5. Add the butter and garlic and cook for 1 more minute. Stir in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add the combined half and half mixture in small splashes, stirring continuously.
  7. Gradually sprinkle in the Parmesan cheese, stirring continuously.
  8. Add the drained pasta and stir to combine. Add the chicken strips and let it warm through for 2 minutes.
  9. Garnish with basil, whole leaves or chiffonade (shredded/finely cut). Serve!
